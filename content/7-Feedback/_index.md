### Feedback on the FCJ Bootcamp Program

Participating in the First Cloud Journey (FCJ) program has been an amazing experience for me. 

**1. Work Environment**
The environment is incredibly dynamic, friendly, and encourages continuous learning. The team is always open to sharing knowledge.

**2. Mentor Support**
I want to express my deep gratitude to the mentors and the admin team. You were always there to answer questions, guide me through difficult architectures, and encourage me to solve problems on my own.

**3. Program Content**
The 12-week curriculum is very well-structured. Moving from basic AWS services to building a complete Serverless CI/CD project gave me hands-on, practical experience that directly aligns with my career goals as a Cloud Engineer.

**Overall Rating:** 10/10. I highly recommend this program to any student passionate about Cloud Computing!
